Who I am

About me

Hi! I'm Gabriele, a software engineer based in Bologna, Italy. I love working at the intersection of software and hardware – from writing bare-metal firmware for microcontrollers to crafting efficient C/C++ code that squeezes every cycle out of constrained devices.

When I'm not hacking on embedded targets I enjoy exploring systems programming, operating-system internals, and the occasional Rust deep-dive. I believe that understanding how things work at the lowest level makes you a better engineer at every layer of the stack.

I'm always open to interesting projects and collaborations — feel free to reach out!
